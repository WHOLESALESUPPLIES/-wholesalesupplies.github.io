- Food wheat is wheat with a high protein content (more than 12.5%), which is used for the production of flour. This wheat variety is of the greatest importance for exports, as it meets the requirements of international markets. In 2021, about 80% of the harvested crop was wheat with a protein content of more than 12.5%, and more than 40% of this volume was wheat with more than 13.5% protein.

Durum wheat (hard grain) is used mainly for the production of pasta. These wheat varieties are also actively exported, especially to countries in Europe and the Middle East.

Spring and winter wheat — Russia actively exports both types of wheat, as they are widely cultivated in various regions of the country, including the Southern and Central Federal Districts.

There is also a classification by class:

The highest is a high—quality and strong grain crop.
The second one is for bakery products.
The third one is suitable for baking.
The fourth is used as an additive to "strong" wheat.
The fifth is feed grain for animal feed.
